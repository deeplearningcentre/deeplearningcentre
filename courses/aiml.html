Machine Learning & AI - Lecture Plan

Explore the concepts and techniques of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence

Course Introduction

Welcome to the Machine Learning & AI course. This course will cover the foundational concepts, techniques, and applications in the fields of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.

By the end of this course, you will have an understanding of key algorithms, methodologies, and how to apply them to real-world problems.

Lecture Topics

  • Week 1: Introduction to Machine Learning & AI
  • Week 2: Supervised Learning - Linear Regression & Classification
  • Week 3: Unsupervised Learning - Clustering and Dimensionality Reduction
  • Week 4: Neural Networks & Deep Learning
  • Week 5: Reinforcement Learning
  • Week 6: Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Week 7: AI in Computer Vision
  • Week 8: Ethics in AI & Machine Learning
  • Week 9: Real-world Applications of AI
  • Week 10: Final Project & Review

Key Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the core concepts and techniques of machine learning and AI.
  • Ability to apply algorithms to solve real-world problems.
  • Develop a foundational understanding of supervised and unsupervised learning.
  • Learn how to implement deep learning models for complex problems.
  • Understand ethical concerns and the societal impact of AI technologies.
