Cassandra Learning Lecture Plan by Deep Learning Centre Gurgaon

  • Monitoring Cassandra with JMX

8. Conclusion and Hands-on Exercise

Time Frame: 1 Hour

  • Review of Key Concepts
  • Hands-on Project (Create a Simple Cassandra Cluster)
